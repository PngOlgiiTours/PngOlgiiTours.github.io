About Kimkim Watchers

KIMKIM PNG (Papua New Guinea) BIRD WATCHERS & TREKKING promotes tourism industry in the upper Nebilyer Local Level Government (LLG) and Western Highlands Province. It carries out tourism activities in the District and the entire Province. Currently the road links from Tomba (local village) up to Teldepangi (local village) vicinities were upgraded and is in good shape.

By researching through, Tambul Nebilyer is one of the unique districts in the province. It has fast flowing rivers, exotic waterfalls, high mountains, wide valleys, very temperate cold climate, and also home of fresh vegetable such as; broccoli, cauliflower, potato, carrot, straw berry, cabbage, sweet potatoes and others.

The indigenous people are very much friendly and receptive to strangers. They are always amazed and loved to see tourist from abroad and nationally.

The district also has two high mountains. The Mt. Giluwe which share borders with Southern Highlands Province and Western Highlands Province. The province of Western Highlands share boarders with Enga Province. These provinces are potential tourism attraction areas and thus without doubt Tambul Nebilyer is embarking on promoting tourism activities.

Tambul Nebilyer has fast flowing rivers like Nebilyer River and Kagul River. These are very attractive sites that has the potential of tourism activities. Such activities like rafting, expedition, kayaking and fishing are very much suitably conducive.

Moreover, Tambul Nebilyer District has unique culture and traditions. For instance, Bride Price ceremonies, pig killings for traditional occassions and festivals such as “Moka Festival”; and cultural performances like traditional fire making. Those activities are indeed breathtaking events that will thrilled visitors who are new and wishing to experience different cultures and traditions.
