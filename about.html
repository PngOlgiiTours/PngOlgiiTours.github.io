About Us

PNG Olgii Tours is a Tourism Product (Tour Operator) aimed at providing experience for tourist/visitors in Papua New Guinea. We intend to make travelling tourists' trip a safer and secure in ensuring that their staying with us will be a lifetime experience.

PNG Olgii Tour (PNGOT) was established to provide superior Tour Operator in the millennium and services/experience to maximise tourist attraction, utilizing available resources by creating more favourable environment for tourist. It has a vision to expand its services to more sophisticated array, magnificent way of attracting tourists.

PNG Olgii Tours has its core values to provide tour package for all tourist at an affordable cost. Safety and security of tourists is our uttermost concern and we are determine to provide the best security.
