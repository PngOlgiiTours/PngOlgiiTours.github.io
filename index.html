Cultural Event

Goroka Show

Goroka show is an annual Cultural event in one of the Highland Provinces of Papua New Guiena. The event is a significant event that showcase different cultures and lifestyles of 22 Provinces of the country. It usually attracts tourists around the world to visit the event.
