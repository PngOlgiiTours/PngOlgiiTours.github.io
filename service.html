Our Services

Tour Guide

We have professional tour guide in all centres to guide you throughout your journey in PNG. They speak English and Tok Pidgin where you can be well assisted.

Hire Cars

The type of Transport to be used depends on the choice of tourists, number of tourists and the condition of the road. Mostly of Tourist choice.

Hotels & Lodges

Hotels, Lodges and Guest House are available for all tourists who visit our site, in all centres of Papua New Guinea. It is either a local style guest house or a fair economic suit hotel room available for all tourists.

Trekking

Papua New Guinea has long been a popular destination for trekking, mountain climbing and bushwalking, and is home to the world-famous Kokoda Track. Trekking in Mt. Hagen is enjoyable as it is on a verge of two rivers small and big (Lgand) flowing sides, Mt. Giluwe Trekking and, Mt. William Trekking.
